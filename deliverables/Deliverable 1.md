## 1. Description of the Business Case

* The app is designed to track biodiversity of all kinds of living beings (animals, insects, plants) in their natural environment.
* Users can capture discoveries during hikes using their phone camera and instantly obtain detailed information.
* This functionality is enabled through a linked database supporting image recognition.
* Capturing observations also contributes to tracking species within their habitat.
* The collected data can:

  * Help monitor species proliferation
  * Detect signs of extinction
* This information is valuable for:

  * Governments
  * Ecological organizations
* The app includes gamification features:

  * Points system
  * Virtual rewards
* These features encourage:

  * Outdoor activity
  * Contribution to environmental preservation

## **6. Main Constraints**

* **Technical Constraints:**

  * Image recognition accuracy limitations
  * GPS precision issues in forests
* **Legal Constraints:**

  * Compliance with privacy regulations
  * Risks of sharing endangered species locations
* **Financial Constraints:**

  * Infrastructure costs (cloud storage, data processing)
* **User Engagement Challenges:**

  * Maintaining long-term activity and interest
